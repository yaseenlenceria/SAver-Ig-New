Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how SaveRig protects your data.

Last updated: December 2024

Information We Collect

SaveRig is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Instagram URLs you submit for downloading
  • Basic usage analytics (anonymous)
  • Technical data for service improvement

We do NOT collect: Personal information, Instagram login credentials, or store downloaded content.

How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used to:

  • Process your download requests
  • Improve our service performance
  • Analyze usage patterns (anonymously)
  • Prevent abuse and ensure security

Data Protection

We implement several security measures:

  • All data transmission is encrypted (HTTPS)
  • No personal data is stored on our servers
  • URLs are processed temporarily and deleted
  • No user accounts or registration required

Information Sharing

SaveRig does not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With trusted service providers (under strict confidentiality)

Cookies and Tracking

SaveRig uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential cookies for website functionality
  • Anonymous analytics cookies (optional)
  • No tracking cookies or advertising cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting basic functionality.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use our service without providing personal information
  • Request information about data we may have
  • Contact us about privacy concerns
  • Stop using our service at any time

Children's Privacy

SaveRig does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of SaveRig after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
